Here is some text inside a blue box.

Things to Try:

  • .inner {position: relative; left: 10px}
  • .inner {position: relative; right: 10px}
  • .inner {position: relative; right: -10px}
  • .inner {position: relative; top: 10px}
  • .inner {position: absolute; top: 10px}
  • .outer {position: relative} .inner {position: absolute; top: 10px}
  • .outer {position: relative} .inner {position: absolute; top: 10px; bottom: 20px;} (and unset the height on .inner)
  • .outer {position: relative} .inner {position: relative; top: 10px}
